Vanilla Chiffon Cake Recipe

Total time: 1 HOUR 20 MINUTES

Author: Lily Ernst

This classic chiffon cake is tender and ligth as air. It has the richness of a butter cake and the fluffiness of a sponge cake.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups (235g) cake flour
  • 1&1/2 cups (300g) granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp (12g) baking powder
  • 1 tsp (5g) salt
    • 7large egg yolks, room temperature 1/2 cup (125ml) vegetable oil 3/4 cup (188ml) cold water 1 tbsp (15ml) vanilla extract 7 large egg whites, room temperature 1/2 tsp (2g) cream of tartar
